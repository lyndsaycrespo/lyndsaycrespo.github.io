A Little More About Me

I am a developer, writer, designer, and project manager with six years of experience in the community development sector, six years of experience as a grant writer, and four years of experience in project management. In my current role, I’ve raised over $13 million in funding to implement cross-sector initiatives in a high-poverty, high-opportunity community.
